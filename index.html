Privacy Policy — Inmobiliaria Desktop

Last updated: November 7, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Inmobiliaria Desktop (developed by Giuliano Caruso) collects, uses and protects personal data when you use the application.

2. Data we collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: email and username if you register.
  • Application data: property records, settings, and other content you store or sync via your company account.
  • Diagnostics: non-personal usage logs and error reports to improve the app.

3. How we use data

Data is used only to provide and improve core app functionality: authentication, property management, local storage and optional synchronization with your company’s cloud database. We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

4. Data sharing and third parties

We do not share personal data with third parties except if required by law or to provide optional services you explicitly enable (for example, integration with a third-party API or cloud provider). Any third-party services used are described in the app and comply with their own privacy policies.

5. Data retention & removal

Data stored locally is retained until the user deletes it or uninstalls the app. For data stored on the cloud (if you enable sync), contact your company administrator for removal. For removal requests, contact tu_email@tu_dominio.com.

6. Security

We follow reasonable security practices to protect data. However, no internet or local storage method is 100% secure—use strong credentials and secure networks.

7. Children

This app is not intended for use by children under 13.
